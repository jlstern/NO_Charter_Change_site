When I ran for alderman in 2013, I was on my own. The mayor, the Newton Tab, almost every elected official, did not support me. I raised a little money and knocked on the door of every voter in Ward 2. I was able to campaign and win without any institutional support, becasue I was running only in the Ward. I think most people consider me to be an independent voice, and I like to think that Newton would like to have more candidates like that. If the proposed charter change eliminates Ward Councilors, I will vote NO on it in November, 2017.

- Emily Norton, Ward 2 Councilor
